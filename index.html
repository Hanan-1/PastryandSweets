Our Story

We started 8 years ago as just an idea. A random impulse decision to open a sweet shop.

We knew there wasn't enough good old fashioned sweet shops anymore. So we opened one in a small indoor market selling sweets.

Back then we weighed out 100 grams of and pre-bagged up the sweets. Which as you can imagine took a lot of time and effort.

But we were determined to bring back a traditional way of doing things those old fashioned sweet shops you remember as a child.
